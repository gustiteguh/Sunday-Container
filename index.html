SHARING SUNDAY

Introducing "Sharing Sunday" Uniting hearts, Empowering change

In the heart of Malmo Sweden, a beacon compassion shines bright as high school students rally together to create a remarkable charity event: Sharing Sunday.Born from a collective desire to make difference in the community, at Sharing Sunday doors swing open wide, welcoming one and all to partake in an extraordinary opportunity to extend a helping hand.

Donate used items or yearning to lend your time and energy as a volunteer!

Sharing Sunday is more than just a collection of donations or a group of volunteers, it is a testament of incredible power of community spirit. It is a reminder that when individuals come together with common purpose, they can move mountains and transform.

So, mark your calendars and spread the word!
